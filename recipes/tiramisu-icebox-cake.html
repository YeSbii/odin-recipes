Tiramisu Icebox Cake

Description

This tiramisu icebox cake is easy, creamy, and just sweet enough. The graham crackers soften nicely, and the coffee and mascarpone are well-balanced. It's an easy, no-bake dessert for hot summer nights.

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, divided
  • 3 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups strongly brewed coffee or espresso, cooled
  • 43 graham cracker rectangles
  • unsweetened cocoa powder, for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine mascarpone, powdered sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla in a large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until fluffy, about 1 minutes. Add cream. Beat until sift peaks form. Set aside.
  2. Combine coffee and remaining 1 teaspoon vanilla in a shallow bowl or pie plate. Working with one cracker sheet at a time, quickly dip each graham cracker into the coffee mixture, allowing excess to drip off.
  3. Arrange 8 1/2 dipped crackers in an even layer in a 9x13-inch baking dish, breaking crackers as needed to fit the dish.
  4. Spread 1 1/2 cups mascarpone mixture evenly over the crackers. Repeat layers of dipped crackers and mascarpone mixture 4 times tp create 5 cracker and mascarpone layers, ending with mascarpone mixture on top.
  5. Cover and chill 4 hours to overnight or until well-chilled.
  6. Let stand 10 minutes at room temperature before serving. Dust generously with cocoa powder just before slicing and serving.
