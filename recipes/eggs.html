Bird's Nest Eggs

Description

These bird's nest eggs are a spin on eggs Napoleon, with crispy hash browns baked into ramekins and filled with savory sausage and melted cheese. Each one is topped with a perfectly cooked egg with a soft, rich yolk for a breakfast that tastes as good as it looks.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded frozen hash browns, thawed and patted dry
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup cooked, crumbled sausage
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 large whole eggs
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Put 2 empty greased ramekins in the oven for 5 minutes to heat up.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium-hight heat. Spread hash browns in a thin layer in the skillet;cook, stirring as little as possible, until golden and crispy.
  3. Carefully remove hot ramekins from the oven. Divide hash browns and carefully press hash browns into the bottom and up the sides of each ramekins. Press firmly. Sprinkle sausage evenly over hash browns;sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Make a small well in the center of each ramekin. Crack an egg into each well. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Carefully return ramekins to the oven; bake for 12 to 18 minutes, based upton how you want the yolk. 12 to 14 minutes will leave a runny yolk; at 15 to 18 minutes, the yolk should be jammy to firm.
