Cowboy Coleslaw

Description

This cowboy coleslaw is a creamy, zesty summer side dish that comes together in just 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16-ounce) package shredded coleslaw mix
  • 1 (15-ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen corn, thawed and drained, divided (from 1[10-ounce] bag)
  • 1 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup seeded and finely chopped jalapeno chile
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 3 tablespoons canned adobo sauce
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning mix
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced scallions
  • lime wedges, for serving

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Toss coleslaw mix, black beans, corn, red bell pepper, cilantro, and jalapeno together in a large bowl until well combined.
  3. Whisk mayonnaise, sour cream, lime juice, adobo sauce, taco seasoning, salt, and cumin together in a medium bowl until smooth.
  4. Pour dressing over the coleslaw mixture and toss until evenly coated.
  5. Garnish with scallions and serve with lime wedges. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for up to 12 hours before serving
